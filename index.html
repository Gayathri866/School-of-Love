School of Love

















Children love without boundaries. They help another up when they fall, they share their candies with someone who’s sad. They are inclusive, encouraging, and their kindness extends towards all.



Children do not worry themselves with the thoughts of tomorrow. They are content living this day to the fullest. Children with disability can also be equal contributors to our society with our love and support to make their life colourful. With your love, Vivent will be able to develop technology tools that will help students to capitalise on their strengths.

Vivent Foundation is intending to develop an interactive learning system with rich content using assistive technologies to improve the skills of differently abled children.

Creating assistive technologies are beneficial because they build human capacity and can be instrumental in enabling people with limitations in functioning to remain in or return to their home or community, live independently, and participate in education,

Title of the Milestone

Our long term goal is to enable a source of income for the differently-abled, and create a Centre of Excellence (CoE) with advanced research and learning facility. We believe our goals will help the differently abled individuals to realise their hopes, dreams and ambitions.

Some of the elements we are trying to build include, text-to-speech and speech synthesising technology for improving numeracy for the children. This will help students to line up words, equations and numbers on their assignments.
